About Le Memoire Event Management Services

Le Memoire Event Management Services is the creative mind-child of youngsters with fresh perspectives who want to ensure that every important moment in life is savoured, and that maximum happiness is provided. We, at Le Memoire, provide event management services for important moments such as betrothals, weddings, birthdays and other recreational events, catered to perfection in order to match your vision. To celebrate happiness and memories, Le Memoire specializes exclusively in recreational events; no corporate events are undertaken here. No matter what stage of planning you are in, we welcome you to let us help you to make your vision a reality.

At Le Memoire, nothing but the best services are rendered to our customers with whole-hearted dedication and an enormous amount of love and affection. With us, you can be assured that your events will be treated as one of our own, and no efforts will be spared to make every event with us joyous and memorable.
