About Le Memoire Eventz

Le Memoire, a pan-India event management and planning service, brings you quality event planning with fresh and unique perspectives. The focus is entirely on the customers’ vision of the event, and therefore, everything will be unique unto you. We offer a wide variety of choices to ensure that our customers get nothing but the best services for their money’s worth. OUR SPECIALITIES

  • Renewal Of Vows

    The 60th, 70th and 80th birthdays are often celebrated to renew the marital vows between the husband and the wife. At Le Memoire, we organize the Sashtiabdapoorti, Bheemaratha Shanti and Sadabhishekam to celebrate the union of a lifetime between people.

  • Thread Ceremonies

    The thread ceremony (punal/ janevu) is a very important rite of passage for young boys. At Le Memoire, thread ceremonies of all cultures are organized.

  • House-Warming/ Inaugrations

    A new house/ work-space marks new beginnings and new adventures for everyone who is closely connected to the space. Le Memoire makes the start of your new journey unforgettable and most importantly, memorable.
