About The Team

The core team comprises of three youngsters who strongly believe in celebrating what is important in life and providing maximum joy to the maximum number of people possible, all while maintaining a fine quality. The idea of Le Memoire sprung up at a tea stall, while discussing a wedding that one of us had attended. What fascinated us was the amount of work that goes into the background throughout the entire event. The memories made at events such as weddings, anniversaries and birthday parties hold a very essential place in everyone’s lives, and we wanted to be able to spread that happiness around. The very thought of bringing someone’s vision to life on their special day, making it all the more memorable, is the very essence of Le Memoire, as the name in itself means ‘The Memory’.
